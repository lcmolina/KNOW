e-publishing

open access

In order to share across the educational sphere and address the right to education by any self-elected means – it is crucial to believe in access for those who are pursuing education outside the boundaries of organized institutions. Education is different in many spaces and independently sought after learning is a journey unlike that of a university student. Injustices to those without access who plan to align with those who not only engage with by way of their institutional access but have often, also, created the platforms on which scholarly publication occurs must be addressed. Open access may be a potential solution to this problem.
